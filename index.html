Nurturing Minds & Shaping Futures.

Gangotri English Boarding Secondary School was established in Chhireshwornath-7 in 2049 BS with government recognition up to Grade 5, and progressively upgraded to 10th standard with government recognition in 2061 BS. Our students consistently achieve excellent results in Board Examinations.

Welcome to Gangotri

Gangotri English Boarding Secondary School is a child-friendly, privately managed institution. Students receive a holistic education rooted in moral values and cultural awareness, alongside CDC-approved curriculum designed to prepare them for local and global opportunities.

Dedicated Teachers

Our qualified and experienced teachers create a sense of community and belonging in every classroom.

Academic Excellence

Gangotri has been awarded the National Certificate of Excellence multiple times for outstanding academic performance.

Best Infrastructure

Modern classrooms, science lab, computer lab, and a conference hall — all at an affordable fee structure.

Student Support

Comprehensive support programmes for academic development, college readiness, and personal growth.

Our Team

Our team is highly experienced and dedicated to providing the highest quality of education. We are committed to ensuring our students get the best possible learning experience in a positive, supportive environment.
