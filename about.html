Welcome to
Gangotri School

Gangotri English Boarding Secondary School, Dhanusha was established in 1992 AD (2049 BS) as a private educational institution situated in Hariharpur, Dhanusha, Nepal. It is affiliated to the National Examination Board (NEB) and approved by the Ministry of Education.

The school offers educational programmes from Pre-Primary to Grade 10, as well as Plus Two (Grades 11 and 12) in the Management and Education streams.

Gangotri provides secondary-level education at a moderate fee structure and also offers scholarships for deserving students.

Salient Features

  • Science Lab & Computer Lab
  • Multimedia Classrooms & Conference Hall
  • Sports, Dance & Music
  • Counselling & Scholarship Schemes
  • Extra-Curricular Activities (ECA) & Educational Tours
  • Safe Drinking Water on Campus

Courses Offered

  • Nursery – Grade 10 Programme
  • Plus Two (+2) Education
  • Plus Two (+2) Management
